Jasmin Ferrera

Jasmin Ferrera has been recording online for over 5 years now amassing an impressive collection of vintage and modern bass guitars to cover all sounds. He also plays upright bass. He is known as a groove player who plays for the song and is proficient in a range of styles and techniques. Recent credits have included playing on demo projects all the way through to Hollywood films, TV and everything in between. His live credits include Katie Melba, Martha Reeves and the Candelas, Les Miserable (West End) and TV sessions with Rod Stewart and Olly Moors.
